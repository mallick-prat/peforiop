Meet Prat & Ethan

Prat Mallick '25

Howdy! I’m a junior in Leverett studying Computer Science and Government interested in non-traditional pathways to public service from technology to entrepreneurship. I currently serve as the Vice-President of the Institute of Politics where I’ve helped to pilot the IOP Public Service Scholarship, create new affinity coalitions, spreadhead investment in student innovation, and streamlined IOP wide logistics to make the Institute more available and accessible to all. I formerly served as the Co-Chair of the Harvard Votes Challenge where I led a robust, campus-wide effort to register 100% of Harvard students for the 2022 midterm elections. I also have continued in my capacity as a member of the Harvard FAS Public Service Standing committee, helping to create new synergy across Harvard’s public service programming, new academic and internship opportunities, and developing the Certificate on Civic Engagement. I have also previously served as a Data Fellow for the Harvard Votes Challenge and Director of Community of the Harvard Public Opinion Project. I’m excited to use 3 years of experience in supporting public service at Harvard to create an IOP that prepares you for the future, bridges communities, and mobilizes for change.

Ethan Kelly '25

Hey y'all! I'm a junior in Lev studying Government. I'm interested in political media, elections, and communications. I currently serve as the Communications Director for the Institute of Politics. In my tenure, I've revitalized our approach to engagement, pioneering website redesigns and compelling student-focused videos that have significantly boosted our digital presence. I take pride in my contributions to amplifying underrepresented voices through intentional social media strategies and initiating the Fellows spotlights, which have transformed student and staff interactions within the IOP. Collaborative at my core, I've worked alongside chairs and staff to elevate our events and support services for students, not least through my co-chairing of the new Gender and Sexuality Coalition. With a hand in everything from launching the IOP’s TikTok to reimagining our communication channels, I am deeply invested in nurturing a community that is connected, engaged, and ever-ready to innovate in the realm of public service.
